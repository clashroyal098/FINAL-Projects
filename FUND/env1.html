I am Dalia Al-Ghalayini from Gaza, from inside the fierce war against us since the start of the war on Gaza on October 7, 2023. Until this moment we have suffered a lot. Our bodies have become exhausted. We drank contaminated water and got sick. My children and I ate very little food. We suffered from the heat of the tent and the presence of insects and reptiles around us. I am very afraid for my children and my family. Danger is all around us, and the bombing, destruction and blood have not stopped. Please help me in this campaign, whether by donating financially or publishing. Thanks to all.
