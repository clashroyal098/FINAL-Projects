About this space

Overlooking lush parkland and Sydney's sprawling skyline, this Rushcutters Bay studio is situated in the ‘Aquarius’ building, designed by iconic architect Harry Seidler. Honouring its designer history, the apartment has been professionally styled with contemporary pieces and offers floor-to-ceiling glass windows boasting uninterrupted views day and night. This peaceful address perimeters the bustling city, surrounded by trendy eateries and just 10-minutes to the buzz of Potts Point. The space This in-vogue apartment has been thoughtfully designed to maximise on space and provide a calming sky high retreat. Select a good read from the bookshelf and recline in the sitting nook by the window under the streaming sunshine or enjoy Netflix and Amazon Prime (own subscription required) from the comfort of the luxe queen bed. The kitchen is sleek with modern light fittings illuminating the stone bench top. An induction stove, oven and small appliances are supplied to ensure you can cook up a storm at home. Bar stools tuck in next to the kitchen making for a convenient breakfast bar, while a neat multifunctional table is nested in for meals or working at home. Fall asleep to the sparkling harbour lights and rise with the sun drenched treetops of the park. Block out blinds can be used for those hotter months and portable heaters and fans are supplied to temper the room. Make yourself at home by utilising the clothing storage with hangers, an iron and a washing machine. Unwind in the large shower recess and enjoy modern tiling, cabinetry and large bright windows. This building is great for outdoor entertainment as a pool and picnic area is onsite for communal use. Take the stress out of city parking as this building provides plenty of secure on-site spots for your vehicle (one vehicle permitted only for the apartment). Guest access During your stay, you and your group will have the entire space to yourself. Please make yourself at home!
