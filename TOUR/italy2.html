About this space

The UNIQUE and UNREPEATABLE VIEW that you will enjoy from this house will make your stay unforgettable! Completely renovated with valuable materials and design elements in 2018, it is located in the oldest part of COLLE DI TORA . This open space of 55 square meters without doors and with large windows has been designed to enjoy in every corner of the BREATHTAKING PANORAMA in which the house is incorporated. The stay experience is designed to offer comfort, relaxation and fully experience the magic of the lake. The space Colle di Tora is a country of Roman origin, where time seems to have stopped. Considered one of the most beautiful “pearls” in Lazio, this village nestled in the waters of Lake Turano offers breathtaking views and magical and picturesque views. Here, in 1900, my maternal grandmother, was born, grandmother Antonietta, was born here. This view was my first view of the world for me, and I still keep memories and scents of it. After so much research, I found this house, which was just a bad, abandoned space, used as a waste storage. No one until then had had the courage to take it and transform it, and everyone thought I was crazy to embark on such a feat! But I immediately imagined it over and saw all its potential beauty. This home has been dreamed of and built with so much love and passion and I am happy to share all of this with you. Lovers of light, relaxation and breathtaking views, we conceived this 55-square-meter apartment, like an Open Space, with no doors , incorporated into the surrounding nature. The white of the house and the natural light that enters from the large windows exalt and frame as in a painting the beautiful landscape of Lake Turano with 180-degree views of Castel di Tora, Antuni and Ascrea.
